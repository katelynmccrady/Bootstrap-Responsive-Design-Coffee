My Coffee Credentials

This is a collective list of the best cups of coffee I have had in the Harrisonburg area in my humble opinion. Here are my credentials...

  • Daily Coffee Drinker: Whether it’s a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon treat, coffee is a staple in my routine.
  • Tried & Tested : I’ve personally visited and sampled coffee from numerous shops in Harrisonburg, making this list based on firsthand experience.
  • Quality Over Quantity : I appreciate a well-balanced cup—smooth espresso, perfectly frothed milk, and rich, nuanced flavors. No burnt, bitter, or watered-down coffee here!
  • Atmosphere Appreciator: Coffee isn’t just about the drink; it’s about the experience. Cozy vibes, friendly baristas, and a well-curated playlist make all the difference.

Here is my list of the best cups of coffee I have had in the Harrisonburg area, based on flavor, quality, and overall experience.

Drifter's Cafe

Why I Love It: A hidden gem in Harrisonburg, Drifters Café serves some of the best espresso in town. Their lattes are smooth, perfectly balanced, and never too sweet. Whether you’re grabbing a cappuccino to go or sitting down with a pour-over, every cup is crafted with care.

Magpie Diner

Why I Love It: Magpie isn’t just about great food—their coffee program is top-tier. Their expertly brewed drip coffee and creamy lattes make it a perfect pairing with brunch. Plus, the cozy, modern diner atmosphere makes every visit feel special.

Broad Porch Coffee Co.

Why I Love It: Located in the Agora Downtown Market, Broad Porch Coffee Co. serves high-quality, ethically sourced coffee with a rich, smooth taste. Their oat milk lattes are some of the best in town, and their commitment to sustainability makes every sip even better.

Greenberry's Coffee Co.

Why I Love It: A classic coffee spot with consistently good brews, Greenberry’s is perfect for a no-frills, high-quality cup of coffee. Their dark roasts have a bold, rich flavor, and their seasonal drinks are always worth a try.

Merge Coffee Company

Why I Love It: Merge Coffee Co. focuses on craft coffee with a community feel. Their espresso shots are pulled to perfection, and their rotating selection of beans keeps things interesting. Whether you're a black coffee purist or a flavored latte lover, there's something for everyone.
